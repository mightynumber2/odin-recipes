Arizona Famous Hawt Dog

Description

That's a lot of ketchup! You dang right it is. That's how an Arizona dog is done, my person. 5 thick lines of pure, unadulterated ketchup runs the length of the hawt dog.

And the rule is: "There can be only one." - Duncan McDog of the Clan McDog.

Ingredients

  • One white hawt dog bun
  • Uno All-American beef hawt dog
  • 1 long squirt of ketchup

STEPS

  1. Toss hawt dog bun on a plate.
  2. Boil or air fry the hawt dog until blisters rip along the entire dog.
  3. Gently set hawt dog into bun.
  4. Squirt 1 long, zig-zagging line of ketchup along the dog.
  5. DO NOT MAKE MORE THAN ONE - THERE CAN BE ONLY ONE!!
