SOS

Description

See Shit on a Shingle in all it's glory! White bread, or biscuits if you prefer, topped with creamy, or gooey, All-American ground beef. Ensure enough salt and peppa, and what you have here is Heaven. Pure, unadulterated love.

Sure, it'll stuff your pipes and assault your organs, but it's worth it. Who wants to live forever when Heaven awaits, eh?

Ingredients

  • White bread or biscuits
  • 2 tbs of real animal (no margarine!) butter (optional)
  • 1 lb ground beefus
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flower power
  • 1 1/2 cup of whole milk - skim is sugary crud
  • Some salt
  • Some peppa, if you prefer, if not - it's okay

STEPS

  1. Bring out your white bread or biscuits.
  2. Butter them, tbs each, if you will.
  3. Get that All-American ground beef sizzling on the stove, medium-high for that sear effect.
  4. Remove ground beef from pan when there's only a few light-pink areas left.
  5. Pour in flour and milk in the same pan, mixing into the hot beef fat. Some say do it slowly, I say: Just Do It.
  6. Lower heat and stir the mixture until your wrist is unhappy and it becomes creamy or gooey. Preference.
  7. Get the ground beef back in there. Mix. Smell it. Dip your finger and taste; then salt and pepper to taste. Then taste again. Remove from pan into a bowl.
  8. Toast your buttered buns of choice in the air fryer. Don't have an air fryer? Then flip them butterside down onto the greasy pan you just used until they are toasted.
  9. Slap toasted buns-of-choice onto a plate and top with creamy beef.
  10. You're welcome.
