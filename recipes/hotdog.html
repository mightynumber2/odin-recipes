Hotdog

Description

The all-American hotdog! All beef weiner in a bun, topped with your choice of condiments! Make sure you wriggle it through your mouth-chomper as you eat it, for good effect!

Ingredients

  • A hotdog from Walmart
  • A bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Some hawtsauce
  • Relish?
  • Maybe onions
  • Anything more is criminal

Steps

  1. Boil, airfry, or grill your dog--DO NOT MICROWAVE
  2. Add to a fresh or stale bun
  3. Top liberally with condiments listed above, and only with those listed above!
