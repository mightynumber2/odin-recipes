Bloody Chicken Wings

Description

Easiest chicken wings you'll ever make. Ketchup. It's all you need in life, let's be honest. Okay?

Are you serious? You damn skippy I'm serious. Never been more serious. This ain't some joke. This is the way.

Ingredients

  • Bag of frozen wings from Walmart. Don't bother cutting up fresh ones from the meat section. Too much work, not enough reward.
  • Ketchup. I'm talking the corn syrup kind. REAL ketchup. None of that "real sugar" stuff. That stuff is weaksauce.
  • That's it. Don't laugh. I mean it.

STEPS

  1. Cook the wings until crispy in either the air fryer or oven. No flipping needed. Trust me.
  2. Remove from cooking source and add to a white plate.
  3. Drizzle in corn syrupy ketchup.
  4. Live your best life.
